...Pancake Recipe...

Learn a skill for life with our foolproof easy crêpe recipe that ensures perfect pancakes every time.

Servings: 8

Cook Time: 15 mins

Prep Time: 5 mins

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • ¼ teaspoon salt, or more to taste
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 ¼ cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons butter, melted

Ingredients

  1. Put 100g plain flour, 2 large eggs, 300ml milk, 1 tbsp sunflower a pinch of salt into a bowl or large jug.
  2. Set aside for 30 mins to rest if you have time, or start cooking straight away.
  3. Set a medium frying pan or crêpe pan over a medium heat and carefully wipe it with some oiled kitchen paper.
  4. When hot, cook your pancakes for 1 min on each side until golden.
  5. Serve with lemon wedges and caster sugar, or your favourite filling.

Bon appetit!
